
"Where's your basket?," the mother asks Felix with a short side glance at the basket Daria dropped next to her chair while rummaging through an arsenal of stirring spoons.

"In my room. The handle broke yesterday," Felix murmurs while chewing on his piece of corn.

Fabian looks at Felix and the lingering question is visible in his eyes. 'Is this okay?,' they ask.
