“I want to be like dad but I really have a bad feeling about this Barney. I think there is something wrong.” Bob looked into Barney’s eyes hoping he would find understanding there, but his brother’s expression was blank. He seemed thoroughly enchanted, his eyes were fixed on the light as if he could see nothing else anymore. He took Bob’s hand and pulled him along as he proceeded towards the light. And even though Bob wanted to resist it, he could feel the beckoning of the light as well.
The eerie song of the nightly forest began to echo all around, but Bob could hardly hear it anymore. The light called to him louder and louder the closer they got, but at the same time, a vague fear crept upon him and he gripped his brother’s hand tighter with every step…
